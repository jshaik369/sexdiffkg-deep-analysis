**Date:** 2026-03-04
**Status:** COMPLETE

## Coverage
- 1,555 drugs matched to ChEMBL indications AND have sex-differential signals
- 1,891 unique therapeutic indications analyzed

## Interpretation
1. **Psychiatric indications** (ADHD, schizophrenia) show extreme female bias — likely combination of exposure and PK differences
2. **Female-specific indications** (amenorrhea, atrophic vaginitis) show 0-3% female bias — complete paradox explained by exposure: women take the drugs, male reports become the signal
3. **Parasitic diseases** (onchocerciasis, strongyloidiasis) are male-dominated patient populations → male-biased AEs
4. **Cancer indications**: Mixed patterns — breast cancer drugs vs prostate cancer drugs create opposite biases
