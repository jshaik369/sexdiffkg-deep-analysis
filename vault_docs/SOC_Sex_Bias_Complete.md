## Paradoxes
- **Cardiac 65.1% female**: Despite male-dominated cardiovascular disease epidemiology
- **Reproductive 34.2% female**: Expected female-biased but actually male-biased (exposure paradox)
- **Eye 32.3% female**: Unexplained male bias in ophthalmic AEs
- **Renal 67.2% female**: Despite similar kidney disease prevalence

## Publication Implications
- First comprehensive SOC-level sex-differential analysis at this scale
- Challenges organ-specific assumptions about sex vulnerability
- Cardiac and renal female predominance are clinically actionable
