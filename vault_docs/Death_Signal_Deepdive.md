## Key Drug Findings
- **Opioids dominate female-biased death**: Oxycodone (80%F), Tramadol (100%F), Hydrocodone (75%F)
- **Antipsychotics**: Olanzapine, Risperidone, Ziprasidone all 100% female-biased death
- **Alcohol/cocaine**: 100% female-biased death signals
- **Benzodiazepines**: Alprazolam, Diazepam all 100% female-biased

## Most Extreme Signal
TRIAMCINOLONE + Death: LR=2.37 (151 female reports vs 13 male)

## Interpretation
1. The female predominance in drug-associated death is NOT just exposure bias
2. Sudden death (94.6%F) and cardiac death (100%F) suggest pharmacokinetic vulnerability
3. Opioid + female death bias aligns with known PK differences (lower body weight, different CYP metabolism)
4. Antipsychotic death bias may relate to QTc prolongation sex differences
5. This is potentially the most clinically important finding in SexDiffKG
