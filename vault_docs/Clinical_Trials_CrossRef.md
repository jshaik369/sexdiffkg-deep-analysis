# Clinical Trials Cross-Reference: SexDiffKG Findings
**Date:** 2026-03-04
**Source:** ClinicalTrials.gov API v2 (MCP tools)

## Executive Finding: MASSIVE GAP IN SEX-DIFFERENTIAL ADR TRIALS

Of our 5 major sex-differential drug class findings, ClinicalTrials.gov contains:

| SexDiffKG Finding | Dedicated Trials | Status |
|-------------------|-----------------|--------|
| Opioid sex-differential ADRs (h=0.526) | **1** (NCT06244706, Belgium, n=150) | Unknown |
| Checkpoint inhibitor sex differences (h=0.463) | **1** (NCT06562777, OHSU INSITE, n=100) | Recruiting |
| Antipsychotic sex-differential metabolic ADRs (h=0.433) | **0** | — |
| ESR1 paradox (tamoxifen in males) | **18** (mostly gynecomastia/male breast) | Mixed |

### Key Trials

**NCT06244706** — QSPainRelief-STRAT
- Sponsor: Université Catholique de Louvain
- Evaluates opioid + non-opioid combinations stratified by age AND sex
- n=150, started Feb 2024
- Only trial directly investigating sex-stratified opioid adverse event outcomes

**NCT06562777** — INSITE
- Sponsor: OHSU Knight Cancer Institute
- INvestigating Sex and Gender-Related Differences in Immunotherapy Treatment Effects
- n=100, observational, recruiting since Sep 2023
- Primary completion: Mar 2026

**NCT00564252** — Topical Finasteride for Hirsutism
- Sponsor: Iran University of Medical Sciences
- n=56, completed
- Only finasteride trial in women with FPHL/hirsutism/PCOS endpoint

## The Gap
- **0 trials** specifically studying sex differences in antipsychotic ADRs despite 70+ years of clinical evidence
- **1 trial** for sex-stratified opioid outcomes despite opioids being the #1 female-biased drug class
- No FDA-mandated Phase IV sex-differential safety studies for any drug class
- Only tamoxifen in males has meaningful trial activity (18 trials) — driven by male breast cancer, NOT by sex-differential safety concerns

## Manuscript Implication
> "A search of ClinicalTrials.gov reveals that despite decades of evidence for sex-differential adverse drug reactions, only 2 active trials specifically investigate sex differences in drug safety for the top 3 sex-biased drug classes identified by SexDiffKG (opioids, checkpoint inhibitors, antipsychotics). Zero trials exist for antipsychotic sex-differential metabolic ADRs. This underscores the need for computational resources like SexDiffKG to systematically prioritize sex-differential signals for prospective validation."
