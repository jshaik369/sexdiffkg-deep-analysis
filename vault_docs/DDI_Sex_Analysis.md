## Interpretation
1. **Indication effect**: Inhaler combos (COPD) = male-dominated patient population → male bias
2. **Opioid combos**: Maintain strong female bias (80%F for oxy/paracetamol)
3. **Buprenorphine+Naloxone paradox**: Used in addiction treatment (more males) → flips the female opioid pattern
4. **AE dampening**: Common AEs (fatigue, diarrhea, rash) show less female bias in combos, suggesting polypharmacy may equalize sex-specific risks
